About Us:

The Professional Brotherhood of Engineers seeks to build leaders out of engineers Texas A&M students.
The purpose of PBE is to create a brotherhood of engineers that thrive socially, academically, and professionally.
PBE will develop engineers for the future by focusing on professionalism through networking, social events and entrepreneurship to become better leaders.
